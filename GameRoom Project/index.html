FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How much is everything? Our game room welcomes everyone with no entry fee, and registered students enjoy free rentals across all games and consoles—just bring your FIU One Card. Faculty and guests can join in the fun at just $8 an hour for unlimited access to our top-tier consoles, board games, and game tables.
Can I bring my own controllers/games/consoles? Dive into our fully-stocked game room, where everything you need for an epic gaming session is at your fingertips, free of charge. To ensure the fun never stops and all our guests have equal access to top-notch entertainment, we ask that personal games, controllers, or systems stay at home. This policy keeps our community playing together, with a world of games waiting for you right here. Come play by our rules and experience gaming at its best!
Can I bring guests? Step into our vibrant game room, where guests are warmly welcomed to share in the excitement. Only registered students, however, hold the key to unlocking our treasure trove of gaming items for free—simply show your FIU One Card. Join us for a play, stay for the community, and let's make every game an inclusive adventure!
How can I host an event? For an impeccably tailored event experience, we invite you to contact our Event Planning Office with your proposal. Our dedicated team is on hand to guide you through a professional planning process, ensuring your event is both memorable and seamless. Engage with us to bring your distinguished gathering to life in our game room's dynamic setting.
